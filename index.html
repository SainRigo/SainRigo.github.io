Project 2

This research developed an AI solution to efficiently sort good and damaged African pears using the YOLOv5 algorithm on a dataset of 2900 pear images. The model achieved a mean average precision of 85.1% and has the potential to aid farmers in choosing high-quality produce, reducing waste, increasing efficiency, and improving farm incomes. This illustrates how merging agriculture and technology can help secure livelihoods and sustainability for African communities reliant on vital crops.
